AJWAF advanced baabs class 2: practice questions
=================================================

Ajawf - Baab Inqaada:



Question. fanhaara bihi.
in'hāra (ٱنْهَارَ). base letters meaning: to collapse
Answer. MaaDiy. conj 1. it collapsed.
This seems to be the only instance of an ajwaf from baab inqaada in Qur'aan.

Ajwaf - Baab Ista'aana:



Question. laa yastateeuwna Darban fil arDi
laa yastateeuwna. base letters meaning: to be able to
Answer. Mudari'. conj 3. they will not be able.

Question: waizaa qara'tal quraana fasta'iz billah
istaiz - base letter meaning: to seek refuge
Answer. u seek refuge! conj #7. amr.

Question: falyastajeebu liy wal yuminuw biy.
li yastajeebuw - base letter meaning: to answer
Answer. conj #3. its in jazm; li yastajeebuw. Amr caused jazm. Active. amr #3. translation: they should answer.
Question: wahumaa yastagheesaaniy
base letter meaning: seek help. Answer. ya = masculine. they two sought help
conjugation: #2 they two seek help
